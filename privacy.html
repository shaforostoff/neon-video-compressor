Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This application (“Neon Video Compressor”) is a utility app to compress and convert video files on your device. We take user privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how Neon Video Compressor handles data.

2. Data Collection and Use

Neon Video Compressor does not collect personal information, device identifiers, usage analytics, or advertising identifiers. It does not have Internet access and cannot transmit your videos, files, or any other data to any server. All video conversion and compression happens entirely on your device.

The app stores your last-used conversion settings (video mode, encoder preset, audio mode, audio bitrate, and quality slider position) in the app's private on-device storage (inaccessible to other apps), purely so your preferences are remembered between sessions. No file content or media metadata is included in this data.

3. Permissions

Neon Video Compressor requests only the permissions necessary to convert videos on-device:

  • Video selection — the app does not request broad media-library permissions. Instead, you pick the video files to convert yourself using the Android system file picker (Storage Access Framework). The app can only access the specific files you select.
  • Output files — converted videos are written directly to your device's shared Movies/Music library via the Android MediaStore, so they appear alongside your other media. Neon Video Compressor never writes outside of this MediaStore location or the files you explicitly chose.
  • Foreground service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PROCESSING, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC) — allows long-running video conversions to continue reliably in the background, with a persistent progress notification.
  • Wake lock (WAKE_LOCK) — keeps the device awake while a conversion is in progress, so long conversions are not interrupted.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS, Android 13+ only) — used to display progress and completion notifications during conversions.

Neon Video Compressor does not request the INTERNET permission and has no network access. All permission-gated operations are performed entirely on your device. No files or metadata are sent to any server or third party.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Neon Video Compressor does not share any data with third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or external services. It contains no ads and no third-party SDKs.

5. Security

Because Neon Video Compressor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or file content off your device, there is no risk of data leakage or unauthorized access related to user data.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
