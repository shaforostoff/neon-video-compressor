Neon Video Compressor re-encodes video files on your device to reduce their file size, using the HEVC (H.265) codec for video and AAC for audio. All conversion happens locally — your files are never uploaded anywhere.

You can choose to re-encode, copy without re-encoding, or remove the video and/or audio track independently, and control quality and speed via the CRF and preset settings.
